Talaqa

Talaqa redefines speech therapy by combining the motivational aspects of gaming with effective therapeutic exercises. The game provides an engaging and interactive experience for young individuals, While the specialist platform ensures progress is monitored and therapy plans are refined. The website was made using Svelte and the game using Unreal Engine 5.
